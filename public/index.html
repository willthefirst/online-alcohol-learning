There are many things you will do in the case of an overdose emergency. Re-order the following steps from 1 (first) to 5 (last).

  1. Offer them water
  2. Contact their FroCo (if they are a first year)
  3. Stay with them until help arrives
  4. Call the YPD: 203-442-4400
  5. Try to keep them talking and awake

Correct! All of these steps are important, but the first thing is always to call for help. Even if you are not sure, call for help!
